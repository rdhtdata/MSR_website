Our Journey.

From a single hydraulic press to a sprawling 22,000 sq. ft. precision facility.

1991 - 1996

The Foundation

Initial MSME setup established with one hydraulic press and lathe machine, building a core foundation in precision manufacturing.

1997 - 2002

Capacity Scaling

Installed high-tonnage press machines and established a dedicated tool room to take on larger, more complex projects.

2003 - 2010

Infrastructure Expansion & Diversification

Purchased 22,000 sqft of industrial land. Diversified heavily into Special Purpose Machinery (SPM) and agro-machinery production.

2011 - 2022

ISO Certification & Fabrication Upgrades

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification. Added heavy machinery such as Shearing and CNC Bending machines to drastically expand our manufacturing scope.

2024 - Present

Assembly Area Expansion

Currently expanding operational workspace from 6,000 sqft to 9,000 sqft, with a major focus on establishing a state-of-the-art dedicated assembly area.
