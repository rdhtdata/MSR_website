About MSR

Built on precision, sustained by operational discipline.

MSR Engineering Works has grown from a focused MSME setup into a trusted manufacturing partner for customers who need dependable engineering execution, responsive service, and practical problem-solving on the shop floor.

Who we are

Since its establishment in 1991, MSR Engineering Works has built a reputation for excellence in precision press tools and components. Our team combines skilled craftsmanship, modern manufacturing strategy, and a commitment to continuous improvement so customers receive reliable, high-quality output aligned to their exact requirements.

Vision 2030 To become a leading name in precision sheet metal manufacturing by expanding with two more technologically advanced manufacturing units and strengthening our position as a reliable, innovation-led engineering partner.
Mission To deliver superior products and services through advanced technology, skilled craftsmanship, timely delivery, strong safety standards, and exceptional value for every customer engagement.

Leadership and team

The company was founded by Mr. T.M. Sundarrajan, whose focus on precision and innovation shaped the business foundation. Today, Mr. Sai Mahesh M S leads the company with a blend of mechanical engineering depth, business perspective, and a continued emphasis on employee welfare and long-term growth.

Growth Timeline

Measured expansion across capacity, systems, and markets.

The company profile shows a steady pattern of capital investment, facility expansion, quality-system development, and diversification into new manufacturing programs.

1991 to 2000

Foundation and capacity build-out

MSR Engineering Works was founded in 1991 and later expanded with high-capacity press machines for larger projects.

2004

Industrial facility expansion

Acquired 22,000 sq. ft. industrial land to increase infrastructure and support larger-scale manufacturing programs.

2010

Diversification into SPM and agro-machinery

Expanded beyond core precision work into special purpose machinery and agro-machinery production.

2017

ISO 9001:2015 certification

Reinforced commitment to internationally recognized quality systems and structured process control.

2018 to 2022

Manufacturing scope broadened

Added machinery including shearing and bending systems to support wider fabrication and sheet metal work.

2024 onward

Assembly-focused expansion

Workspace expansion from 6000 sq. ft. to 9000 sq. ft. is underway, with a dedicated assembly area in focus.

Community And Sustainability

Engineering work that extends beyond factory output.

  • Conceptualized, designed, and delivered as a practical public-use infrastructure project.
