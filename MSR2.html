About MSR Engineering Works

Founded in 1991 by Mr. T.M. Sundarrajan, MSR Engineering Works has evolved into a premier ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturer of precision press tools, press components, sheet metal fabrications, SPM, and agro-machinery. Based in Bangalore’s Bommasandra Industrial Area, our state-of-the-art facility spans 22,000 sq.ft, equipped with advanced machinery to meet diverse manufacturing demands.

Today, led by Mr. Sai Mahesh M S (Mechanical Engineer, PESIT & MBA, Leeds Business School), we blend modern strategies with traditional values, driving innovation, employee welfare, and sustainable growth. Our team of 25+ professionals (avg. 12 years experience) ensures defect-free production with rigorous QA processes.

Vision 2030: Establish 2 more advanced manufacturing units, leading in precision sheet metal solutions.
